Step 1: Choose your Origin Story

Build A Superhero

The origin story is where your hero is from. It explains who your hero is, where they came from, and what inspired them to become a hero. To start this off, think about an event or experience that could be what inspired them to become a superhero. It can be a thirst for revenge on someone or something that wronged them, or an urge to help people. It could be that they fell in a vat of radiation or they were born on a different planet. It doesn’t have to be tragic at all, but it should be important. A strong origin story connects your hero's powers, values, and goals.
